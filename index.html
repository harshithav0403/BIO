Quality Control

Responsible for testing, documenting and releasing all materials and products as per cGMP.

Key Activities

  • Method validation & verification
  • Routine release testing
  • OOS / OOT investigation support
  • Analytical data review & approval

Primary Tests

  • Assay & purity
  • Impurities profiling
  • Microbiological testing
  • Environmental monitoring

Key Documents

  • Approved specifications
  • STP / SOP library
  • CoA templates
  • Change control records

Raw Materials

Ensures all incoming materials meet defined quality, safety and traceability requirements.

Scope

  • APIs & excipients
  • Packaging components
  • Solvents & process aids
  • Reference standards

Controls

  • Vendor qualification
  • Sampling plans (AQL)
  • ID & purity testing
  • Quarantine vs. release status

Outputs

  • Material approval / rejection
  • Material traceability logs
  • Vendor performance trends
  • Deviations & CAPA tracking

In-Process Control

Monitors critical steps during manufacturing to ensure consistent product quality.

Monitoring

  • Critical process parameters
  • IPC sampling & testing
  • Yield & mass balance
  • Line clearance verification

Compliance

  • BMR / BPR review
  • Deviation handling
  • Equipment qualification
  • Training & competency

Dashboards

  • Batch-wise IPC results
  • Process capability (Cp, Cpk)
  • Trend charts
  • Alerts & action limits

Stability

Designs and manages stability studies to support shelf life and storage conditions.

Study Types

  • Accelerated & long-term
  • In-use & stress studies
  • Post-approval commitments
  • Ongoing stability

Chamber Management

  • Chamber qualification
  • Temperature & RH mapping
  • Alarm & excursion control
  • Backup & disaster plans

Reporting

  • Stability protocols & reports
  • Shelf-life justification
  • Regulatory submissions
  • Product quality reviews

Documentation

KPIs
